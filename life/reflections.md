# Reflections
_Thoughts worth keeping_

## April 2026

Time feels like it's running away at 34-35. Research says this happens because life shifts to autopilot — fewer novel experiences proportional to total life lived. The fix isn't productivity, it's presence + novelty + relationships.

Key reminder: the dying don't regret unfinished to-do lists. They regret not being present with people they loved.

Munger's version: stop watching yourself live. Read more. Do fewer things but do them well.
