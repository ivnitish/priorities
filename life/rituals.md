# Rituals & Novel Experiences
_Things to build with Vyshnavi and family_

## Every Month
- [ ] One new restaurant neither of us has been to
- [ ] Cook one new dish together at home
- [ ] One printed photo from the month — stick it somewhere visible
- [ ] "Best moment of the month" — each name one, write it down

## Every Quarter
- [ ] One day trip somewhere neither of us goes routinely
- [ ] One "state of us" evening — 1 hour, no phones. What's working, what's been hard, what do we want next 3 months to feel like

## Every Year
- [ ] Birthday letters to each other — what you noticed, what changed, what you're proud of. Keep them.
- [ ] New Year's eve question ritual — same questions every year: What was your favourite day? What surprised you? What are you most grateful for? Write answers, date it.
- [ ] One curated printed photo book for the year
- [ ] "This year we..." list — one evening in December, list everything we did together

## Ongoing
- [ ] Record parents/elders talking about their life and memories — do this soon
- [ ] One trip per year that is genuinely new territory for both of us
